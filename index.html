About

About this site

This website is used as a database to keep track of what projects I listen to such as full-length albums, extended plays, and mini albums. I love to listen all kinds of music and always love to explore more!

The only condition in which a project will be put into this website is that if and only if it is listened in the same year it was released to keep relevancy.

  • For example, if I discover an album in 2024 that was released in 2023, that album will not be inputted into the website.
  • If I discover an album in November 2024 that was released in January 2024, it will be eligible to be put into the website.

Spotlight

Song of the Month

KID AGAIN - Jon Bellion

Jon Bellion is one of the music industry's leading songwriter/producer having written and produced for many artists such as Tori Kelly, Jung Kook, Jonas Brothers, and countless others. So when I heard he was releasing his first original music since 2018's "Glory Sound Prep" and 2021's "I FEEL IT" with Burna Boy, I became so ecstatic. Jon Bellion, for many years, have been one of my main inspirations for songwriting and producing. His passion for the craft of music is shown in is "The Making Of..." videos where he is filmed through his process of making a record, which has inspired me to create music myself. His love for the craft of music has drawn the attention of millions of people to not only his music but the music he has created for other people. As for this single, "KID AGAIN", he talks about being a "kid again", being released from his previous recording contract, feeling like he can do anything under his own terms, his own control. This song is pure ear candy for any Jon Bellion fan, but also shows how much he has grown since his previous releases, further maturing his sound and his lyrics. Nevertheless, this song is still a Jon Bellion song through and through. Hey, hey, the king, the legend, is back.

  • Released September 27
  • Rating: 9/10
