About

About this site

This website is used as a database to keep track of what projects I listen to such as full-length albums, extended plays, and mini albums. I love to listen all kinds of music and always love to explore more!

I mostly find new music through social media advertisements, recommendations from friends, and Spotify recommendations. Hopefully this browser helps you find new music to listen to!

Spotlight

Song of the Month

Internet hometown hero - MICO

I've been highly anticipating this project from MICO ever since the first single, "HOMESICK" came out when he brought on former guitarist and producer for Valley, Mickey Brandolino. Mickey's magic is heard throughout this project, showing that he is passionate in his craft. MICO on the other hand, his songwriting shines through and through along with the upgraded production. This project is no-skips, front to back. The Filipino-Canadian's craft only becomes finer with each project, showcasing his growth to become a much bigger artist.

  • Released October 25, 2024
  • Rating: 8.5/10
  • Favorites: The one to fall, Senses, Tears in your eyes
